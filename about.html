About the Contest

Welcome to the "Lens & Light Photography Contest 2025" - a celebration of creativity, perspective, and storytelling through the camera! This contest invites students from all departments to showcase their passion for photography and capture the beauty that often goes unnoticed.

Our Mission

Whether it's the calm of nature, the emotions in a portrait, or the energy of street life - every shot tells a story. Submit your best shot and let your lens speak the words!

We believe that photography is not just about capturing moments, but about seeing the world through different perspectives and sharing those unique viewpoints with others.

What We Stand For

Creativity

Encouraging unique perspectives and innovative approaches to photography.

Inclusivity

Open to students from all departments, fostering a diverse community of artists.

Excellence

Recognizing and celebrating outstanding artistic achievement and technical skill.

Passion

Nurturing the love for photography and visual storytelling among students.

Theme: "Nature Through the Lens"

Landscapes

Capture sweeping vistas, majestic mountains, serene lakes, and dramatic skies that showcase nature's grandeur.

Wildlife

Document the fascinating world of animals in their natural habitats, from birds in flight to creatures in their daily routines.

Macro Details

Explore the intricate details of flowers, insects, textures, and patterns that reveal nature's hidden beauty.
